🎯 Getting Started

Frame to Form uses a 3-step pipeline to convert your images into 3D assets:

  1. Generate or Upload: Create an image with AI or upload your own
  2. Remove Background: Clean up the image automatically
  3. Convert to 3D: Transform into a production-ready GLB file

1. Generate Image

Use AI to generate images from text descriptions, or upload your own reference photos.

2. Remove Background

Automatically remove backgrounds with pixel-perfect precision.

3. Convert to 3D

Transform your images into production-ready 3D models (GLB format).
